DENTAL VENEERS

A dental veneer is a thin piece of porcelain that is used to enhance or change the appearance of your natural teeth. It is directly bonded to your tooth and can help repair chips, cracks, uneven spacing, and other natural tooth defects. Dental veneers can even be used to make your teeth appear straighter and whiter.
